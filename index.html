Will I Sleep Warm?

A backcountry sleep-system checker

I've spent too many nights curled up shivering because I trusted a bag's rating and forgot the cold creeping up through a thin pad. So I built this. Punch in what you're actually bringing and it'll estimate how cold you can sleep comfortably — and tell you the one thing to change if the numbers don't add up. It's an estimate, not a guarantee, so always leave yourself a margin.

How you sleep

Read this: this is a rough estimate built on rules of thumb, not a lab. Bag ratings, your metabolism, altitude, hydration, and food all move the real number. When it's genuinely cold, being wrong is dangerous — build in a margin, carry a backup layer, and know the signs of hypothermia.
